About

Stephanie King is a savant and cognitive scientist available as a human subject for scientific research in neuroscience, cognitive science, computational neuroscience, and quantum cognition laboratories.

Research Breakthrough

Resolved the Mental Rotation Paradox in cognitive science—invalidating the allocentric model of spatial cognition, a theoretical framework that failed to constrain mental rotation despite decades of acceptance in the field.

This breakthrough addresses a fundamental paradox in how humans process spatial information and challenges prevailing cognitive science orthodoxy through comprehensive computational analysis of 50+ years of research literature (280+ papers, 1970-present).
